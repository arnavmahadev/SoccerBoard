World Cup 2026 Forecast

A statistical model rates each team's strength and runs 10,000 tournament simulations to figure out who's most likely to win. It updates as games are played. Injury and squad news is checked by hand and the numbers are adjusted when something significant comes up. Adjusted teams are flagged so you can see exactly what changed and why.

Group stage

Accuracy

Backtested on 2,546 international matches the model had never seen during training. What matters is not just whether it picks the right winner, but whether its probabilities are actually accurate.
